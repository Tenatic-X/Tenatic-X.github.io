### Timeseries Naive model forecast

There's something I learned with experimentation importance, is always start with a simple model as a baseline to compare to with other experimentations. A simply way to create a naive model for any timeseries data, is using the previous value, as the future value. Aka if bitcoin price yesterday is $5000, then tomorrow will also be $5000.

Prediction model that has entirely relied on random non predictable results, will have an extremely difficult ability to beat that naive model. This intrinsicly makes sense because of how randomization works. There's almost an equal amount of chance to predict closer to the true future, as it is to predict further. Essentially neutralizing both, aka recreating naive model.
