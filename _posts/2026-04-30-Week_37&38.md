### The bot problem of airfare sites
Obviously, most sites prevent bots from accessing their webpage. So there needs to be some loop holeing to go around the bot issues. `Ctrip` required signing into an account to mostly access their airfares, but further blocked me from airfares searching after multiple use on an account. So I used random SIM cards I have access to but they block after completing one day of scraping. I've chosen to actually not scrape `Ctrip` because it provided higher airfares than `Momondo`.

`Momondo` rate limits when refreshing the pages of airfares too often - the easiest circumvention is using the +-3day from flight day, and extract the price displayed in the grid with the dates that were valid in the selection (14, 15, and 16 day lengths). Loading takes much longer, and reduces refresh by up to 19x, allowing to reduce rate limit. However `Momondo` has thrown 'error' page, needing me to manually copy paste the link again to get the page to load.
